# DraggableStage

The `DraggableStage` component wraps a `Stage`, allowing its viewport to be panned around and zoomed.

<Canvas>
  <DraggableStage
    width={400}
    height={400}
    viewportBounds={{ x: 0, y: 0, width: 1000, height: 1000 }}
    smoothScaling={true}
    minZoom={0.1}
    maxZoom={20}
  >
    <Layer>
      <Rect width={1000} height={1000} fill="cornflowerblue" />
      <Circle x={100} y={100} radius={50} fill="green" draggable />
      <Circle x={200} y={200} radius={40} fill="green" draggable />
      <Circle x={300} y={300} radius={30} fill="green" draggable />
      <Circle x={400} y={400} radius={20} fill="green" draggable />
      <Circle x={500} y={500} radius={10} fill="green" draggable />
      <Rect width={1000} height={5} fill="orange" />
      <Rect y={1000 - 5} width={1000} height={5} fill="orange" />
      <Rect width={5} height={1000} fill="orange" />
      <Rect x={1000 - 5} width={5} height={1000} fill="orange" />
    </Layer>
  </DraggableStage>
</Canvas>
